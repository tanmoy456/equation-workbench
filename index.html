The Google Slides add-on

Insert equations onto a slide without leaving Slides, then come back and edit them later — the source travels with the image.

  1. Open a presentation, then Extensions → Equation Workbench → Open editor.
  2. Type LaTeX, click a slide, and hit Insert into slide.
  3. Later, select an equation and Read selected to load its source back in.
  4. Edit, then Replace selected to swap it in place at the same spot.

Your data stays with you

The web editor runs entirely in your browser. The Slides add-on only touches the presentation you have open, and only when you ask it to. Nothing is sold, and nothing is stored on our servers.
